# Top 200 Companies Hiring in India — Data Scientist | ML Engineer | AI Engineer | MLOps

> **India-first guide.** All salary figures in INR (LPA = Lakhs Per Annum). Work mode reflects India office policy. Interview processes based on candidate reports from Naukri, LinkedIn India, Glassdoor India, Blind, AmbitionBox, LeetCode Discuss, and Interview Query — updated 2025–2026.
>
> **Key context:** India is the world's second-largest hub for data science talent. Over 1,600 Global Capability Centers (GCCs) operate in India. India's AI/ML job market grew 60%+ since 2019 with 84,000+ active DS/ML openings. Bangalore, Hyderabad, Pune, Gurugram, Mumbai, and Chennai are the primary hiring cities.
>
> **Salary benchmarks (INR, 2025–2026):**
> - Fresher / 0–2 yrs: ₹6–14 LPA
> - Mid-level / 3–5 yrs: ₹15–30 LPA
> - Senior / 6–9 yrs: ₹30–60 LPA
> - Staff / Lead / 10+ yrs: ₹60–120 LPA
> - Principal / Architect: ₹1 Cr+ (top MNCs and product companies)
>
> **Difficulty ratings:** 🔴 Hard · 🟠 Medium-Hard · 🟡 Medium · 🟢 Approachable
>
> **Work mode abbreviations:** WFO = Work From Office · H = Hybrid · R = Remote-friendly

### India-specific Interview Preparation Advice

**Pharma analytics roles (Axtria, ZS, IQVIA, Fractal):**
- Case interviews are non-negotiable — practice structured problem solving (Sales Force Effectiveness, HCP segmentation, brand analytics)
- Domain knowledge in pharma commercial: prescription data (IQVIA APLD), HCP targeting, market access, promotion response models
- Python + SQL + PowerPoint is the full stack needed
- Communication to non-technical stakeholders is tested explicitly

**Indian product unicorns (Swiggy, Zomato, PhonePe, Flipkart):**
- LeetCode Medium-level is typically sufficient for coding rounds
- SQL is tested hard — window functions, subqueries, business analytics queries
- ML system design must use India-specific examples: hyperlocal delivery, UPI fraud, food recommendation
- Product sense matters even in DS/ML rounds — understand user metrics and KPIs

**MNC GCCs (Google, Microsoft, Amazon, Goldman):**
- Same global bar as headquarters — no India discount on difficulty
- Leadership Principles (Amazon) are tested with same rigor as US interviews
- For Google Hyderabad / Bangalore: LC Hard is expected; no shortcuts

**Quant Finance (D.E. Shaw, Optiver, Goldman Strats):**
- Math aptitude and probability puzzles are the first filter (e.g., Optiver 80-in-8 mental math test)
- Stochastic processes, Bayesian statistics, and statistical ML depth required
- Target IIT/IISc network for referrals — a significant percentage of quant hires come through these networks

- LinkedIn Jobs (primary for product and MNCs)

- hirist.tech (tech-focused, good signal-to-noise for ML roles)
- Instahyre (AI-matched job platform, popular with startups)
